Spirit of 76 Fireworks - Accounting Intern
Processed payments with high attention to accuracy and detail using QuickBooks software, served as the sole accountant for the Spirit 2022 fireworks tent. Created accurate financial documents, analyzed reports for the finance director, and conducted inventory audits to improve accuracy and efficiency. Provided data-driven recommendations for order quantity and pricing of tent products.
What did I learn?
Overall, my internship at Spirit of '76 Fireworks provided me with valuable hands-on experience in the day-to-day financial processes and operations. Collaborating closely with the Tent Manager, we focused on optimizing orders, minimizing labor costs, and maximizing revenue. By meticulously pricing items and continuously tracking and analyzing sales data, I ensured that our revenue and associated profit consistently exceeded our goals.

Throughout the selling period, my diligent tracking and auditing efforts helped identify errors and corrections, allowing for timely adjustments and ensuring precision and accuracy in our financial reports. The daily and weekly audits were instrumental in maintaining the integrity of our financial data.

As the internship drew to a close, I took on the responsibility of creating a comprehensive final report that encapsulated our achievements. With confidence, I presented this report to the ownership and executive-level management, highlighting the significant increase in sales and profit that our pricing and inventory optimization strategies had achieved, boasting double-digit growth.

By successfully contributing to the financial success of the project, I not only honed my skills in financial analysis and reporting but also gained a deeper understanding of the importance of collaboration, attention to detail, and adaptability in a dynamic business environment. I am grateful for the opportunity to have played a part in driving the growth and profitability of Spirit of '76 Fireworks.

Accomplishments
  • Contributed to double-digit tent revenue and profit.
  • Record-high revenue with an unpredictable market.
  • Reported findings to executive leadership.
  • Calculated discounts and their affect on reaching sales goals.
  • Maintained strict observance to accounting procedures.
  • Implemented and scanned hundreds of Fireworks into the '76 Dealer Network system.
  • Learned and trained others on the '76 Dealer Network system.
Information Technology Intern
Provided proactive deskside technical support to ensure uninterrupted operations and maximized productivity. Maintained and updated computer systems to verify efficient performance, collaborated with deskside IT teams to troubleshoot and implement effective solutions.
What did I learn?
My internship with the IT deskside team at Veterans United Home Loans was a transformative experience. I had the opportunity to work on various projects that allowed me to enhance my technical skills and broaden my knowledge of the industry. One project involved deploying and configuring computers for employees, where I gained hands-on experience with hardware setup, software installation, and troubleshooting. Additionally, I provided technical support to end-users, assisting them with hardware and software issues, network connectivity, and IT system navigation. These experiences taught me the importance of problem-solving and customer service in an IT support role.

Collaborating with the IT team on system upgrades and maintenance tasks was another highlight of my internship. I learned how to perform software updates, ensure data backups, and monitor the overall health and performance of the IT infrastructure. This exposure gave me insight into the critical role IT plays in supporting an organization's operations and the significance of regular maintenance and monitoring

What made this internship truly exceptional was the incredible team at Veterans United Home Loans. Working alongside talented, hardworking, and supportive individuals was a privilege. They provided guidance and mentorship, fostering an environment of open communication and collaboration. I was encouraged to ask questions and seek help whenever needed, accelerating my learning and making the experience the perfect experience for me.
